Info:

I am a professional qualified Italian Chef, creative and enthusiastic, with a genuine passion for food for the past 18 years. I have worked in a variety of culinary settings which has made me a versatile and knowledgeable Chef, and have held numerous leadership positions including Sous Chef and Head Chef. My particular area of expertise lies in Italian and Modern Fusion cuisine.

I have experience in preparing, cooking and serving high quality food in accordance with menus, ensuring consistency and attention to detail. My vast experience in creating and developing menus has also supported a number of previous venues to reach sales targets and strengthen business.

I am highly organised and have a large amount of experience in the planning and management of kitchen operations including overseeing appropriate receiving and storage of food, meeting food cost targets, daily and weekly forecasting, ordering and stocktake. I have particular strength in managing and facilitating a safe workplace in accordance with HACCP procedures and practices.
